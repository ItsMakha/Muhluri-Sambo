About the
Author

Muhluri Sambo is a 24-year-old Author, Activist, Motivational Speaker and Livestock Investor from South Africa best known for the award-winning book, Infinite Teen.

His journey began in Gidjamhandeni village (Malamulele, Limpopo) and later in Glenridge (Protea Glen Extension 16, Soweto). From humble beginnings to global stages, Muhluri represents the voice of a generation determined to redefine leadership and consciousness.
